This page contains the text-only project summary for the Instacart customer behavior exploratory data analysis. Reviewer comments and external project review markup are intentionally excluded.

Overview

Explored an Instacart customer behavior dataset to identify purchasing patterns and basket trends. I cleaned and transformed the data, analyzed order frequency and product preferences, and created visuals to explain customer segmentation. This analysis provides actionable insights for optimizing promotions and improving customer retention.

Key Elements

  • Data cleaning and preparation
  • Customer purchase pattern analysis
  • Feature engineering for behavior insights
  • Visual storytelling for business decisions

Business Impact

The analysis is designed to support business decisions around promotions, product recommendations, and customer targeting. It helps teams understand which customer segments are most valuable and where retention efforts should be focused.
