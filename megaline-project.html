Project Summary

This page contains the text-only project summary for the Megaline Phone revenue analysis. Reviewer comments and any external review details are intentionally excluded.

Overview

Analyzed Megaline Phone company revenue and customer behavior across phone plans to reveal key drivers of profitability. I examined plan usage, churn risk, and revenue per customer while generating clear statistical summaries. The findings help inform pricing strategy and identify high-value customer segments.

Key Elements

  • Revenue and plan performance analysis
  • Customer behavior segmentation
  • Statistical analysis and model validation
  • Results oriented toward business decision-making

Business Impact

The analysis supports business leaders in understanding which phone plans drive the most revenue, which customer types are most profitable, and where improvements to retention and pricing can have the greatest effect.
